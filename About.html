Hello, We are Beat the Bar

Our goal is to promote a Healthy & Fit lifestyle which includes a Healthy diet, Exercise & Strength training by raising awareness about how rewarding it can be. Come join us & Test your strength with our “Hanging Challenge.” Participants attempt to “Beat the Bar” by holding themselves up on a pull up bar while a timer counts down the seconds. If you can outlast the timer, You Win the prize worth $100. Men are timed at 120 seconds (2 minutes) & Women are timed at 90 seconds. Can you Beat the Bar?
